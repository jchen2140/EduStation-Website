OUR MISSION

EduStation aims to mend the gap between privileged and disadvantaged students by providing a variety of resources ranging from programs to scholarships and a community tab where students can share opportunities! By fostering an inclusive space, EduStation hopes to assist students in their journey through high school and beyond!
